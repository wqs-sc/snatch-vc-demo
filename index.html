Core Player Journey

  • 1. Discover: The player sees a holographic Nike sneaker floating in their camera view, pinned to a specific geofenced location in Soho.
  • 2. Approach: Tapping the item opens the proximity tracker. The distance meter shows how many meters away the prize is. The player must walk closer.
  • 3. Snatch: Once within range (< 25m), the glowing "SNATCH IT" trigger becomes active. Clicking it locks the reward.
  • 4. Wallet: The reward lands in the wallet with its corresponding redemption barcode/code, driving the user directly in-store or online.

Quick Hunt • Social Loop (C2C)

The creator loop of Snatch. Quick Hunts are community-created drops. Creators launch viral challenge bounties, campus contests, and scavenger hunts for their crew, driving high-velocity social competition and organic network growth.

Quick Hunt Flow Stages

1. Configure Details

Creator specifies name ("NYC Mystery Crate Bounty"), reward ("Epic Mystery Crate"), squad restrictions, and viral drop limits.

2. Geotag & Drop

Creator opens their camera, locks the 3D holographic mystery crate into physical space, and leaves a location clue ("Under the iron arches").

3. Invite & Rally

App generates a unique QR Code and join code ("NYC99X"). Friends scan to join the live squad lobby instantly.

4. The Squad Hunt

Hunters follow the clue, trace proximity targets, scan the physical location, and snatch the prize.

C2C Creator Loop

Brand events seed the economy, but user-generated Quick Drops fuel daily active network volume. One creator launching a bounty invites active crews of 5-20 players, establishing self-replicating viral growth.

Snatch Mode • Core Gamification

The progression system. Snatch Mode is the always-on gaming layer that transforms walking into routine loot runs. Hunters climb regional ranks, maintain daily streaks, and stockpile legendary gear.

Progression Mechanics

  • Elite Ranks & Seasonal Prestige: Earn XP on daily scans. Leveling up increases hunter prestige and grants access to high-tier drop nodes.
  • Tactical Sensor Upgrades: Spend earned credits on radar amplification and loot detection mods to track rare crates from further away.
  • Epic Vault Keys: Unlock titles like "Alpha Pathfinder" or "Mythic Raider" that secure guaranteed slots in exclusive creator bounties.

Daily Routine Gamification

An always-on loop of daily streaks, territory rankings, and seasonal leaderboards. This keeps players engaged and moving in physical space between major brand drops.
